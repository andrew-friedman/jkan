ABOUT

The National 311 Data Portal includes 311 and service request datasets for the 50 largest US Cities. Through centrally locating the 311 datasets, large scale analysis between cities is significantly easier. The site also includes a case study of Boston's Mobile Sharps Team using 311 Data, and information related to missing 311 datasets.
